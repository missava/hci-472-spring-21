We are a hypothetical user experience and research agency. Our story is personal and focuses on the people. Many years of working in large organizations and cubicle desks often led to great ideas being missed or overlooked. So many of those who are closest to the work have been left out of the discussion. We imagine if we took the time to hear those single voices, there would be great stories and ideas behind those walls. Ideas that can change lives and foster great product innovation. Cubicle 3 takes the time to connect and solve real world problems. So, let us pause and find out what happens in Cubicle 3.
